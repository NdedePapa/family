What is "Generation"?
It tells the tree how many steps below Nana Aku a person is. Gen I = Nana Aku, Gen II = her children, Gen III = her grandchildren, and so on. When you add a child, the generation is filled in automatically — you don't need to change it.
What is "Parent"?
This links the person to their mother or father in the tree. Always select a parent so the person appears in the right place. If you're unsure, leave it blank and edit it later.
Can I add Nana Aku's own mother or grandmother?
Yes! Click Nana Aku on the tree, then press ▲ Add Ancestor. The form will guide you. Her ancestors appear above her at the top of the tree in purple.
How do I find someone quickly?
Type their name in the search bar at the top. Matching people will glow — everyone else will fade out.
The tree is too small / hard to read
Use the ＋ / − zoom buttons on the right, or scroll/pinch on your device. Press ⊙ to fit the whole tree on screen again. You can also drag to move around.
What does "Added By" mean?
It's where you put your own name — so the family knows who contributed each record. This helps when information needs to be checked or corrected.
🔒 What is Admin Mode?
By default, everyone can view and add members. To protect the tree, Edit and Delete require admin access. Click the 🔒 lock button in the top bar and enter the password to unlock admin mode.
What if I spot a mistake but can't edit?
Click the person's card and press 📝 Request Change. Describe the correction needed — the admin will receive your request and can make the fix.
☀/🌙 Can I change the appearance?
Yes! Click the ☀ sun/moon button in the top bar to toggle between light and dark mode. Your preference is saved automatically.
